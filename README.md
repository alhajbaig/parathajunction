# Paratha Junction Website

## Overview

Paratha Junction is a food business website created to solve a real-life problem faced by customers while ordering food. Many people struggle to find clear menu details, prices, availability, and an easy way to connect with local food businesses. This website provides a simple and user-friendly platform where customers can explore the menu, learn about the business, and place orders easily.

The project focuses on improving customer experience through a clean design, smooth navigation, and easy accessibility.

---

## Features

* Modern and responsive website design
* Easy navigation for customers
* Digital menu display
* Business information and contact section
* Mobile-friendly interface
* Fast and simple user experience

---

## Problem Solved

Traditional food ordering methods can often be confusing and time-consuming for customers.
This website helps by:

* Providing all food details in one place
* Making menu browsing easier
* Helping customers quickly connect with the business
* Improving online presence for the food business
